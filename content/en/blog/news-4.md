The cryptocurrency market continues to develop rapidly, and 2024 promises new interesting trends. One of the key areas is decorative financing (DeFi), which provides users with new opportunities for lending and earning on cryptocurrencies without intermediaries. Another significant trend is the growing popularity of stablecoins such as USDT and USDC, which provide price stability against the background of the volatility of other cryptocurrencies. At the same time, active development of blockchain technologies such as NFT and blockchain games is expected, which will open up new opportunities for investment and profit in the market.
